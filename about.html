Meet our Trainers

Megara, a balanced trainer

Megara has always been a sportsperson. Starting from a young age, she was involved in sports in school. She started with athletics then moved on to amateur teams where she found her love of coaching.

Since then, Meg became a fully qualified Professional Trainer who wants to help people find the balance between mind and body. Her speciality is to design training that meets every client uniqueness.

"I aim to help others find balance between mind and body." Meg

Jack, the coach

Jack started his trainer career over 10 years ago. Throughout this time he had the chance to test many different approaches and work with different levels of training development.

He is an expert in helping beginners overcome obstacles while avoiding injuries. Helping them become more knowledgeable and independent in the gym.

"It's mission acomplished if a new member is not afraid of learning new exercises and get healthier." Jack

Spinning

Focus on weight loss and fat-burning. These spinning sessions are designed to allow you to push yourself to new fitness levels. In a group class design to have fun while you get lean.

45 minutes sessions, available all week.

Powerlifting

Is a weight lifting session for a full-body workout. Perfect you are like to lift weights and get in shape while doing it. The session is composed of light to moderate weights with high repetition, and short resting intervals.

45 minutes sessions, available all week.

Circuit Training

Is a half an hour HIT(high-intensity training) workout. The focus is to build cardiovascular fitness and lean muscle. This follows a daily planned circuit with dumbbells, weight plates and bodyweight exercises to blast all the whole body muscle groups.

30 minutes sessions, available all week.
